Pigs on a Blanket

Description

Basically it's sausages cooked in essentially Yorkshire pudding.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp melted butter
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 lb of bangers (or pork sausage links)

Steps

Make batter:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour with the salt and a pinch of pepper
  2. Make a well in the center of the flour
  3. Pour in the eggs, milk and melted butter into the well and whisk into the flour until smooth
  4. Cover and let stand 30 minutes

Pre-heat baking dish:

  1. Coat the bottom and sides of an 8x12 or 9x9 ceramic or metal casserole dish with vegetable oil (use high smoke point rice bran oil or canola oil)
  2. Place a rack in the bottom third of the oven
  3. Put the empty dish on the rack
  4. Preheat the oven with the dish in it to 425°F(220°C)

Brown the sausages:

  1. While the oven is coming to temperature, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a skillet on medium high
  2. Add the sausages and brown them on at least a couple sides
  3. When the sausages have browned and the dish in the oven is hot, pull the oven rack out a bit, put the sausages in the casserole dish and pour the batter over the sausages

Bake:

  1. Bake at 425°F(220°C)for about 20-30 minutes or until the batter is risen and golden
  2. Serve at once
