Cheese Bacon Toast (for breakfast)

Description

A simple, easy go-to for the picky eaters in the family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup finely crumbled cooked bacon
  • 1 loaf sliced French bread (1 pound)

Steps

  1. Place bread slices on an ungreased baking sheet
  2. Top with shredded cheese and partially pan fried bacon
  3. Bake at 425° (or broil) for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown>
  4. Dip in ketchup
